## Particularities

### Stock numbers are sacred
A wrong stock number here can mean overselling on `webstore` and a customer getting "we ran out, sorry" after they paid. Same for `pos` showing in-store availability. Accuracy is the whole point of this app.

### Daily-sales import (integration 02)
Once a day each `pos` instance uploads a sales batch. We deduct stock based on that batch. **Idempotency is critical**: see `apps/inventory/debugging/2026-04-stock-drift.md` for what happens when it isn't. Always check `external_batch_id` before applying.

### Prices flow out, never in
Inventory is the single writer of prices. `webstore`, `pos`, and `admin` all consume prices via integration 01. If `admin` wants to change a price, it goes through the inventory API — not directly into the database.
